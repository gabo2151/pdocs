# Privacy Policy - Pudú Watchface

**Last updated:** April 19, 2026

This privacy policy describes how the **Pudú Watchface** for Wear OS devices handles information.

### 1. Data Collection
Pudú Watchface **does not collect, store, or transmit** any personal data, device identifiers, or sensitive user information.

### 2. Permissions and Sensors
The application accesses the following data solely for real-time display on the watch face:
* **Step Counter:** To display your daily physical activity.
* **Battery Status:** To display the remaining power level.

This data is processed exclusively on the local device and is never sent to external servers.

### 3. Third-Party Services
This application does not use third-party SDKs, analytics services, or advertising platforms.

### 4. Contact
If you have any questions regarding this policy, please contact the developer through the contact information provided on the Google Play Store.
