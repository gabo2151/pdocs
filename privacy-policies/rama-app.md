# Privacy Policy - RaMA

**Last updated:** November 19, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how **GGalilea** ("we", "our") handles your information when you use the mobile application **RaMA** ("the App").

### 1. Introduction
RaMA is designed as a private, utility-focused application. Your privacy is a priority, and we maintain a transparent approach to how the App functions.

### 2. Data Collection and Usage
RaMA is a strictly **local-only** application.
* We **do not** collect, transmit, or store any personal or sensitive user data on external servers.
* All data created within the app (reminders, times, and preferences) is stored solely on your device's internal memory.
* If you uninstall the application, all this data is permanently deleted from your device.

### 3. Device Permissions
The application requests the following permissions to ensure its core functionality:
* **Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS):** Used exclusively to send you the reminders you have scheduled.
* **Exact Alarms (SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM):** Used to ensure reminders trigger at the precise calculated time, bypassing system battery-saver restrictions.

These permissions are **never** used to collect data regarding your location, identity, or device activity.

### 4. Children’s Privacy
Our application does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13, as no data is ever transmitted from the device.
